🗳️ Server Election Rules

  1. Each member must give his/her vote.
  2. Voting shall only be conducted on the official Website of VerseKingdom and not on WhatsApp or Minecraft.
  3. Candidates must register through Website registration form.
  4. Votes are cast using Voter ID and Minecraft Username.
  5. Any malpractice results in temporary ban.

🇺🇳 Powers of the Elected King

  • The elected is titled "King" and forms a government.
  • Can arrest, execute, and timeout citizens.
  • Responsible for citizens' hunger, wealth, housing.
  • Controls Neutral Land.
  • Holds power for 3 weeks.
  • Citizens can overthrow misuse of power.
  • Can add/remove 1 law per week.
  • Can collect tax and improve the Kingdom.

🪙 Currency & Exchange Rate

  • 10 Iron = 1 Diamond
  • 1 Diamond = 40 Coal
  • 10 Iron = 2 Emerald
