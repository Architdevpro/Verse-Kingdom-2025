📜 The Constitution of Verse Kingdom

Preamble

  • We, the citizens of Verse Kingdom, unite under the eternal monarchy of the King, the Supreme Head of the Nation.
  • Our goal is to secure justice, equality, survival, prosperity, and adventure for all players.
  • We ensure order through the Government, fairness through law, and strength through unity.

Part I: The Union and Its Territories

  • The Verse Kingdom is one united server under the King.
  • Lands are divided into:
    • Neutral Land – controlled by the King.
    • Player Territories – owned by citizens.
    • Royal Land – belonging to the monarchy.

Part II: Citizenship

  • All registered members of the server are citizens.
  • Guests are not citizens unless registered via the official website.

Part III: Fundamental Rights

  • Right to equality – all players are equal.
  • Right to survival – freedom to build, explore, and live.
  • Right to protection – safe from griefing and stealing.
  • Right to justice – citizens may appeal under the King’s judgment.

Part IV: Directive Principles of Server Policy

  • Maintain peace, fairness, and economic growth.
  • Protect Neutral Lands such as spawn, farms, and shared builds.
  • Encourage teamwork and survival cooperation.

Part IVA: Fundamental Duties

  • Respect the King, the Government, and fellow players.
  • Protect community lands and structures.
  • Follow server rules faithfully.
  • Report misconduct, cheating, or griefing.
  • Contribute to the growth of the Kingdom.

Part V: The Government

The King (Supreme Head of Verse Kingdom)

  • The King is not elected and rules for eternity.
  • The King is the Chief Judge and final authority on laws, governance, and economy.
  • The King may add, remove, or change any law at any time.
  • Every law must be signed by the King to become valid.
  • The King may dismiss the Prime Minister and Council at any time.
  • The King may take over any territory, property, or function of the server.
  • The King controls Neutral Land and the Bank.

The Prime Minister & Council

  • Elected once every 15 days by the citizens.
  • The Council is formed by the Prime Minister’s party/team.
  • Responsibilities:
    • Protect citizens’ wealth, hunger, and housing.
    • Manage public resources.
    • Propose and enforce one law per week (requires King’s approval).
  • May suggest policies for the Bank and economy, but final authority rests with the King.

Judiciary

  • The King is the Chief Judge of the Kingdom.
  • The King may appoint Server Admins as judges to assist in justice.

Part VI: Economy & Finances

The Bank of Verse Kingdom

  • The Bank is the supreme authority of the economy but is controlled by the King.
  • The Bank regulates:
    • Currency (Rupees ₹)
    • Taxation
    • Trade and player transactions
    • Player accounts and balances
  • The Prime Minister may suggest policies, but the King makes the final decision.
  • The Bank is independent in daily operations but bound to the King’s will.

Currency

  • The official currency is Rupees (₹).
  • Currency may exist in different denominations (notes/coins).
  • The Bank may issue updates on exchange rates, salaries, or rewards.

Taxation

  • Taxes may be collected by the Government.
  • All tax laws must be signed and approved by the King.
  • Taxes are used for public builds, defense, and survival improvements.

Wealth & Economy Growth

  • Citizens may trade freely unless restricted by law.
  • The Bank may regulate shops, trading posts, and currency circulation.

Part VII: Elections

  • The Prime Minister and Council are elected every 15 days.
  • Voting happens only via the official Website.
  • Each citizen has one vote verified by Voter ID + Username.
  • Election malpractice leads to a temporary ban.

Part VIII: Language

  • The official language of Verse Kingdom is English.

Part IX: Emergencies

National Emergency

  • Declared in case of major griefing, server crash, or invasion.
  • The King assumes full control of all functions.

Government Breakdown

  • If the Prime Minister or Council misuse power, the King may dismiss them.
  • The King assumes direct control until new elections are held.

Financial Emergency

  • Declared if the economy collapses or inflation destroys the currency.
  • The Bank (under the King) may:
    • Freeze accounts
    • Reset balances
    • Enforce emergency taxation

Other Powers

  • The King may declare special emergencies (famine, housing crisis, wars).
  • During emergencies, all powers belong to the King.

⚔️ Final Note

  • The Verse Kingdom is a monarchy under the King, who is the Supreme Head of Nation, Judge, and Protector of Neutral Land and Economy.
  • The Prime Minister and Council serve for 15 days at a time, but the King’s word is law above all else.
